New Furnished Townhouse at The VUE at College Square – 4BR with Private Baths
Private furnished bedroom with private bathroom available in a newly built 4-bedroom townhouse just 5 minutes from Liberty University.

The bedroom comes fully furnished with a bed, mattress, desk, and chair, making it ideal for students or young... professionals looking for a comfortable and move-in-ready living space. Each bedroom also includes its own private en-suite bathroom and ceiling fan for added comfort and privacy.

Residents share a spacious and furnished living room with sofas and a TV, creating a great space to relax or spend time with housemates. The home also includes in-unit laundry with a washer and dryer, allowing residents to do laundry conveniently from home.

The kitchen features modern appliances and ample cabinet space, perfect for cooking and meal prep.
Additional features include:
- Private bedroom with private bathroom
- Ceiling fan in the bedroom
- In-unit washer and dryer
- Furnished common living room
- Two designated parking spaces plus additional nearby guest parking

New construction with modern finishes
The townhouse is located just 3 minutes from Walmart, Sam’s Club, and major shopping centers, and only 5 minutes from Liberty University, making it an ideal location for students.
